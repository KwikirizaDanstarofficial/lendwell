FEATURES

Everything a modern SACCO needs

From member management to regulatory compliance — SaccoOS provides every tool you need to run your cooperative efficiently.

Member Management

Onboard members with profile photos, ID cards, certificates, and bulk Excel import. Track groups, guarantors, and member lifecycle.

Loan Origination

Full loan lifecycle — apply, verify, approve, disburse, settle. Dynamic interest rates, amortization schedules, and automated contract PDFs.

Savings & Deposits

Regular and fixed-deposit savings accounts. Automatic interest tracking, account locking, loan offset, and member passbook generation.

Reports & Analytics

Real-time dashboards with KPI widgets. Loan distribution, savings trends, fine reports. Export to PDF and Excel.

SMS Notifications

Automated SMS alerts via EGO SMS API. Loan approvals, payment reminders, and bulk messaging with smart retry logic.

Payments & Mobile Money

Integrated MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money collection. Automated loan repayments, share deposits, and payment reconciliation.
